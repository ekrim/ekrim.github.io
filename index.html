Welcome

Hi! My name is Evan Kriminger and I have been a machine learning researcher at Leap Motion since February 2016. In Fall 2015 I received my Ph.D from the Computational NeuroEngineering Lab at the University of Florida.

Some of my current and past research interests are computer vision and object tracking, recurrent networks, state representations for reinforcement learning, constrained clustering, and active learning. Lately I have been interested in improving network visualization and developing intuitive GUIs for data manipulation, and have been playing around with d3.
